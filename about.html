About Us

Empowering children through the gift of education.

Who We Are

Shiksha Education Foundation was founded with a vision to transform lives through education. What began as a small initiative by a group of passionate educators has now grown into a nationwide movement to uplift underprivileged children by giving them access to quality education, mentorship, and life skills.

Our Mission

Our mission is to bridge the educational divide in India by providing free learning resources, organizing commnunity-driven programs, and building partnerships with schools and NGOs to reach every child who deserves a better future.

Our Impact

  • Educated over 10,00,00+ children in rural and urban slums
  • Partnered with 50+ schools and local communities
  • Organized 200+ workshops, health camps, and awareness programs

Meet Our Team

Our dedicated team of educators, volunteers, social workers, and donors work tirelessly to ensure that no child is left behind in the race to a better tomorrow.
